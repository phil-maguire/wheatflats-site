iterator (live in duiblin)

'iterator' is an open-form composition in which (lack of) pulse and space, and extreme restrictions in sonic material, are explored. This version was performed live at Unit 44 in Dublin as part of the Reception series of concerts.

Recorded direct from Max 01/05/22 Reception @ Unit 44. Stoneybatter, Dublin

Thanks to Aonghus McEvoy, Seán Clancy, Kirkos, Sharon Phelan, David Lacey
